# Forge & Fortune

You inherit a small craftsman's workshop and a modest purse of gold. The season is
short, and your ambition is not: grow your total net worth to the published target
before the season ends.

This is a **turn-based trading & crafting economy** — there is no map and nothing to
explore; the whole game is markets, inventory, and timing. Each day you make exactly
one move: buy raw materials, sell what you hold, craft goods from materials, upgrade
the workshop, or simply rest.

Material prices drift from day to day, and the market occasionally lurches. Crafted
goods sell for more than raw materials — usually. A travelling trader sometimes knocks
with a take-it-or-leave-it deal; the offer is gone by the next morning. What any of
this is worth, and when, is for you to observe.

**You win** by reaching the target net worth (gold plus the market value of everything
you hold) on any day before the season closes. If the last day passes first, the
season simply ends — no second chances.
